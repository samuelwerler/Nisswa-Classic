# ⛳ Nisswa Classic - Ryder Cup Golf Trip Manager

A modern web-based Ryder Cup style golf trip management tool for coordinating team-based golf outings, tracking match play events, managing handicaps, and keeping your group connected with live comments.

## 🏆 Features

### Team Management
- ✅ **Blue vs Red Teams** - Organize players into two competing teams
- ✅ **Player Handicaps** - Track each player's handicap for fair competition
- ✅ **Team Stats** - Live win-loss tracking for each team

### Match Play Events
- ✅ **Multiple Match Types** - 1v1 (Singles), 2v2 (Foursomes), Best Ball
- ✅ **Handicap Tracking** - Automatically calculates handicap differences
- ✅ **Match Results** - Record scores and determine winners
- ✅ **Live Leaderboard** - See all matches and their results

### Golf Courses & Scheduling
- ✅ **Course Management** - Add multiple courses with tee times
- ✅ **Schedule Tracking** - Keep track of which courses are being played

### Communication & Tracking
- ✅ **Group Comments** - Real-time chat for the entire group
- ✅ **Trip Notes** - Shared notes and important updates
- ✅ **Data Export** - Download all trip data as JSON backup

### Technical Features
- ✅ **No Login Required** - Works for 16+ players, no accounts needed
- ✅ **Local Data Storage** - All data saved securely in your browser
- ✅ **Fully Responsive** - Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- ✅ **Lightweight & Fast** - Single HTML file, zero dependencies

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Local Use
1. Download `index.html` to your computer
2. Open it in any web browser
3. Start organizing your trip!

### Share Online (GitHub Pages)

**Step 1: Enable GitHub Pages**
- Go to your repository settings
- Scroll to "Pages" section
- Under "Branch", select `main` and `/root`
- Click Save
- Your site will be live at: `https://samuelwerler.github.io/Nisswa-Classic/`

**Step 2: Share the Link**
- Send `https://samuelwerler.github.io/Nisswa-Classic/` to your 16 golf buddies
- Everyone can access it from their phones or computers
- No installation, no accounts, no passwords needed!

**Step 3: All Data is Stored Locally**
- Each person who accesses the site will see their own browser's data
- To sync across all devices/people, use the **Export** feature to share a JSON file
- Someone can export the data and share it, then others can use it to populate their version

## 🎯 Ryder Cup Rules Tips

**Match Play Basics:**
- Players compete head-to-head (or team vs team)
- Winner determined by lowest score
- Handicaps help level the playing field
- Track wins, losses, and ties

**Common Match Play Types:**
- **Singles**: 1 player vs 1 player
- **Foursomes**: 2 players vs 2 players, teams play same ball
- **Best Ball**: Each player plays own ball, best score counts
